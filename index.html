What is this?

If you love New Orleans and you love soccer, than that's what this is. Growing up I played soccer through high school. When I was going of to college I thought that I had lost my passion for the game, and for a while I had. Whether it was lack of care given to my body as I grew, frustration with the passion of my teammates, or annoyance with how soccer in our state is viewed, I grew apart from soccer. One day I was asked to coach the LSU Women's Club Soccer Team. I agreed. Through coaching I gained my love of the game back. I made incredible friends. I learned a lot through having to communicate a vision and tactics. Now, I look to continue that spark further. I have huge dreams about what this could become. In my biggest hopes, this will one day be used to provide enrichment in the lives of many. For now, I'm just one person, with nothing backing me and no idea how I'm going to take the next step forward, but Im going to try.
